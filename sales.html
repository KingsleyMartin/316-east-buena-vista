Notable Ownership Patterns

  • Longest tenure: Francis Wilson & Charlotte Parker Wilson (46 years, 1909-1955) - Original owners who commissioned the Prairie-style design
  • Second longest: William R. Federici & Elsie M. Federici (37 years, 1958-1995) - Maintained the property through significant period
  • Third longest: Aaron Michael Waldron & Chantal Marie-Claude Combes (12 years, 2008-2020)
  • Total transfers: 11 documented transfers over 116 years
  • Average ownership period: Approximately 11.6 years (excluding current owners)
